SiteDrop AI

Getting Started
★
SiteDrop AI — Main Platform
Describe the client's business, generate a mockup instantly — no coding required
→
How to Use It in Sales
Sales leverage move: Before or during a sales call, plug the prospect's business info into SiteDrop AI and generate a quick mockup. Show them what their site could look like — this turns an abstract pitch into something visual and tangible. Published previews deploy to Vercel (not sitedrop.ai subdomains). The interface is straightforward: type a description, generate, and refine.
